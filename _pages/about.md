I am **Dr. Abdul Rahim V. C.**, Assistant Professor in the Department of Communication Engineering, School of Electronics Engineering (SENSE), VIT Vellore, India.

My research interests include:

- Wireless Communications
- Cognitive Radio Networks
- Spectrum Sensing
- Automatic Modulation Classification
- D2D Communications
- NOMA Systems
- Signal Processing for 5G/6G Networks
- Machine Learning for Wireless Systems

I received my Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Trivandrum.

I actively work on problems related to efficient spectrum utilization, trustworthy wireless systems, and intelligent communication networks.
